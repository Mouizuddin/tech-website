Diya Sharing Foundation


Our objective is to provide holistic empowerment to underprivileged families by offering quality education to children and provide employment opportunities to families

  • Mission and Vision The mission of Diya Foundation is to provide education to children from disadvantaged backgrounds. We envision a world where every Indian child has equal access and opportunity to succeed..
  • Who We Are Diya Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) organization in the United States and holds 80G status in India. Established in 2014 by Sundar Sethuraman, we serve local communities and champion educational initiatives across India. Our primary focus is on rural education, but we also work to uplift underprivileged communities and support various initiatives to ensure sustained human well-being

Our Impact

Over the years, Diya Foundation has touched the lives of thousands of individuals. From building infrastructure in rural schools to establishing computer labs, sponsoring children's education, and providing humanitarian aid during disasters, our initiatives have made a meaningful difference. We believe in the power of collective efforts to bring about positive change and uplift communities.
